What you're hearing

Above Chicago's sidewalks and streets, Chimney Swifts chatter overhead – a declining aerial insectivore that occupies the city's vacant chimneys during migration and breeding seasons. In addition to the Chimney Swifts, you can hear the calls of the Chipping Sparrows, and very distantly, the chirping of House Sparrows.
