Five soundscapes from the region's distinct ecosystems.

Five places. Five mornings. Five soundscapes from the Chicago region's distinct ecosystems — a downtown rooftop, a lakefront prairie, a restored tallgrass plain, a former slag dump turned marsh, and an old-growth woodland. What you hear is what these landscapes sound like now, and what they could sound like if we keep designing for them.
