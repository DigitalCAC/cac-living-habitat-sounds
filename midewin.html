What you're hearing

At Midewin National Tallgrass Prairie, a diversity of sparrows thrives amongst the perennial grasses and wildflowers during migration and the breeding season in the summer. Species like the Henslow's Sparrow can spend the summer at Midewin while other species, like the Le Conte's Sparrow and White-crowned Sparrow breed farther north and stop at Midewin during migration.
